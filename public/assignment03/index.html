This is a game called "Eat Bubble" where you avoid the enemies' attack and try to eat as many bubbles as possible. There are three difficulty levels to choose from -- level 1 being the easiest (most bubbles) and 3 the hardest (fewest bubbles). The user could adjust the difficulty level based on personal preferences at any time There's also a cheat mode, where you can get rid of all the enemies and get focused on eating bubbles only. The game will document your personal highest record and keep track of the bubbles eaten for the current round, as well as the amount of lives you still have for this particular round. Have fun with this game :) Inspiration from M.C. Escher!

Instructions: use the arrow keys to move the player. The player is the white bacteria whereas the enemies are the violet ones. Avoid the enemies and eat as many bubbles as you can!
